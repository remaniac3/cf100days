NUSANTARA ISEKAI

It has been decades since The Great War. In their victory, the immortal Bunian have used their control over sihir to reshape the world of Nusara into an ecological paradise. The Hewani, a collection of beast-like sentient races shaped by sihir, now live in sparse colonies across the Bunian civilization, their once-celebrated diversities now reduced to whichever use their Bunian rulers deem valuable. It is an unstable peace, and revolution is brewing.

Coffy

The Artistic Summoner from Another World

A digital artist and Tea's older sister, Coffy was accidentally summoned into the Bunian capital of Aretamus by Mi who was trying to summon Tea.

Hailed as a mistake, but forced to cooperate, she was blessed with a magical pen forged from Bunian sihir that allows her to turn whatever she draws into life and was promised a safe passage home if she fulfils her duty.

Together with Mi, she must answer the call to restore balance to Nusara.

Tea

The Beastmasked Inventor

A mechanical genius and Coffy's loving younger brother. When Coffy disappeared, he invented a portal into Nusara to find her. Unfortunately, landing in the Hewani colony of Galak was not helpful as he was mistaken for a Bunian.

The Hewani were initially impressed by his otherworldly inventions. Ultimately, the intervention of its panda ruler was what elevated Tea to be accepted as a peer in the inventor colony.

He has since started traversing Nusara with the Hewani, hiding his face behind a mask and helping them wherever he can, hoping to find Coffy before the war begins.

Mi

The Tsundere Bunian Princess

To many within the Bunian empire, Mi is the perfect princess. Her love for dance has made Mi a Bunian celebrity, a point of pride even to her cousin, YinYong. Unbeknownst to most, Mi is a supporter of the Hewani. Mi sought to summon a champion to help her. The spell, revealing the handsome Tea, distracted her greatly, causing her to mistakenly summon Coffy instead.

Now, stuck with the artist from another world, Mi has to try and figure out how one princess and one summoner is to save Nusara from war.

YinYong

Michael

The Wise Sultan of Galak

Rescued as a cub by a blue-haired Bunian, Michael was always more focused on the welfare and future of the Hewani - a colony of craftsmen. His people, thankful for his leadership, have crowned him the Shadow Sultan, the symbolic center of the Hewani revolution.

His true purpose when he met Tea is to be able to journey to Aretamus to thank Mi for her good deed all those years ago.
