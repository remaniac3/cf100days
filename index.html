Comic Fiesta 100 Days Countdown

Artwork by Jean_0v0

Determined to survive and find a way back home from a dilapidated alternate world, Coffy suggested her school music band, with members of Tea, Yin Yong, Mi, and Michael, to host a music festival. Music had always been their passion, and she believed it would boost their spirits and give the students stranded there the will to persevere.

With newfound hope, they set out to plan the festival, all the while wondering how they ended up in this mysterious world and if they would ever find their way back home.

How the story unfolds is entirely up to your imagination!

Comic Fiesta 100 Days Countdown (CF100Days) is a yearly event where hundreds of artists pool their talent each year, creating artworks based on our mascots (Coffy, Tea, Yin Yong, Michael and Mi) every day for 100 days as we count down to the one of the most epic events of the year, Comic Fiesta!

If you submit your artwork before 1st November 2023 (Wednesday), you will stand the chance being selected as one of the 10 Committee Selection and receive an awesome Comic Fiesta 2023 Goodie Bag!
