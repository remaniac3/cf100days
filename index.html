In an alternate Taisho era, Comic Fiesta is a traditional stage performance troupe with a long history. They currently have themselves based at a theatre building that comes with state-of-the-art equipments. However, a cunning businessman opened a new movie theatre down the street, showcasing a revolutionary film medium that threatens the very relevance of the traditional theatre.

As ticket sales of the theatre shows plummet, the performers face a critical choice: Can they save their theatre through hard work and innovation? Or should they collaborate with the businessman?

The fate of the theatre is in your hands!

Comic Fiesta 100 Days Countdown is a yearly event where hundreds of artists pool their talent each year, creating artworks based on our mascots (Coffy, Tea, Yin Yong, Michael and Mi) every day for 100 days as we count down to the one of the most epic events of the year, Comic Fiesta!

If you submit your artwork before 1st November 2024 (Friday), you will stand the chance being selected as one of the 10 Committee Selection and receive an awesome Comic Fiesta 2024 Goodie Bag!
