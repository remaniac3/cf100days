Comic Fiesta 100 Days Countdown

Illustration by kiyo木又 / RYNISYOU

Comic Fiesta-dori, a street suspended in a recent past, is where the Hyakki Yakou (百鬼夜行), the night parade of a hundred demons, conduct their nightly business.

But this is no quiet procession.

Each night, their arrival is announced by the thunderous roar of revving engines, echoing through a cityscape alive with flickering neon, pulsing city pop beats, and yōkai out for a night of mischievous fun. Here, the past is never dead — it just got louder.

How the night unfolds is entirely up to your imagination!

Comic Fiesta 100 Days Countdown is a yearly event where hundreds of artists pool their talent each year, creating artworks based on our mascots (Coffy, Tea, Yin Yong, Michael and Mi) every day for 100 days as we count down to the one of the most epic events of the year, Comic Fiesta!

If you submit your artwork before , you will stand the chance being selected as one of the 10 Committee Selection and receive an awesome Comic Fiesta 2025 Goodie Bag!
